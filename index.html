This is an exciting arcade game in which you take on the role of a penguin who wants to learn how to fly. The plot of Learn to Fly begins in the cold and harsh conditions of Antarctica. Here, all the animals are accustomed to life on land and water, but dream of freedom, like their relatives – birds. The main character, Flint, is dissatisfied with his way of life and decides to conquer the sky. He does not pay attention to the fact that everyone around him tells him that it is impossible.

First Steps to a Dream

The first levels of the game are dedicated to Flint’s preparation for making his idea a reality. Players control the hero, who uses his natural instincts and resourcefulness to get into the air. At first, he tries to fly with the help of simple devices, such as homemade hang gliders and parachutes, which he assembles from scrap materials. Each new time in Learn to Fly becomes a challenge.

For this reason, you should consider:

  • Speed
  • Wind Direction
  • Launch Angle

Success Is Near

Gradually, our new friend begins to realize that he needs more serious means for the perfect result. Users get a chance to improve their equipment by earning points. In Learn to Fly, they can be spent on new flying machines. Each new one not only adds variety to the game, but also opens up new mechanics and possibilities.

Flint moves from a simple hang glider to more complex and interesting designs. Soon, the penguin begins to rise higher and further. However, his path is not without obstacles. At each level, fans are faced with various challenges in Learn to Fly. You need to learn to maneuver and avoid these dangers so as not to lose precious points and time in the game.

These can be:

  • Snowstorms
  • Storms
  • Ice Blocks

Failure or Victory?

During the game, you can interact with other characters who help the hero in his quest. Each new acquaintance brings with it unique stories and tasks, which makes Learn To Fly more intense and interesting. The culmination is the moment when he, having collected all his knowledge, decides to organize a grand flight.

He organizes a competition, where everyone demonstrates their skills and achievements. This is not only the final test, but also a chance to show what you have learned in the Learn To Fly game. Participants will have to control birds in this challenge, taking into account all the skills they have acquired. Get inspired by this amazing story and draw the right conclusions!
